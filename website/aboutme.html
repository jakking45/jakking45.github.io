About Me

Hi, I am Jack. I have spent a-lot of my time working in and around the industry of programming.

In 2016 I became a full-time in-house web developer, I was taken on as an apprentice and spent my time learning as much as I could. I learnt a great deal of new useful transferable skills, I soon descovered that web dev just wasn't my calling in life so I began working on building games. I think it's safe to say I have found that calling now.

I have learnt many aspects of game design and development in my years self learning, at College and now University. Among my experiance is asset creation be it 3D, 2D, modeled or pixel art. I started by creating modifications for my favorite games and progressed to creating my own ones and becomming preficient in industry standard game engines such as Unity and Unreal.

I am also proficient in many programming languages;

  • C++
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Jquery
