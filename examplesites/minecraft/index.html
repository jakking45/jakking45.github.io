Minecraft is a game about placing blocks and going on adventures. Explore randomly generated worlds and build amazing things from the simplest of homes to the grandest of castles and even whole worlds! Play in creative Mode with unlimited resources or mine deep in Survival Mode, crafting weapons and armor to fend off dangerous mobs. Do all this alone or take the challenge of Survival with friends. What will you bring to the land of Minecraftia, the only limit is your imagination.
